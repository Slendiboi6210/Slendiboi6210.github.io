Overview

Problem

In my dorm for my freshmen year I had an issue of needing my phone for my alarm but it was way to far out of reach and would leave a cord wrapped around me when I would sleep which is not comfy. enduring this issue was just not gonna work for me so I resorted to printing a solution

Brainstorming

To come up with a solution I looked at what the phone holders critera for me were. For me that would be preventing the cable from wrapping around me, keeping the phone in reach and keeping the phone secure so it doesnt fall
